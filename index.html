Food is life!

Early human nutrition was largely determined by the availability and palatability of foods. Humans evolved as omnivorous hunter-gatherers, though the diet of humans has varied significantly depending on location and climate. Ok, enough of fancy words. Simply speaking, for most of our history we had to eat what was available, but nowadays most of people have a plethora of options.
Food is now more than just a requirement for survival: it became art! Nowadays, creative and intelligent people from all around the world deisgn incredibly tasty and impressively creative dishes, and some of them even like to compete among themselves. Well, given how amazing this is, it would be sad if other people couldn't at least get their feet wet in this sea of interesting and fun cooking.
Therefore, this website has the mission to act as your own online daily (or almost) cookbook, presenting not only amazing new dishes but also bringing interesting topics in the form of articles. Now let's stop talking and get cooking!
